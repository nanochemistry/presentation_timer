About & Usage

The dark screen overlay tracks elapsed time clockwise from the top.

  • Total time (Format: HH:MM:SS): Total duration of the timer. The elapsed time is indicated by a dark overlay and the background of the timer is green. A sound is played when the times is elapsed.
  • Warning time (Format: HH:MM:SS): Time when the background of the timer turns to yellow and a warning sound is played.
  • Alert time (Format: HH:MM:SS): Time when the background flashes as a visual indicator that the timer is ending.
  • Minutes Only Mode: Rounds up time and anchors seconds display to `:00` to prevent speaker distraction.
  • The warning and and alert sounds can be muted by clicking the sound icon .
